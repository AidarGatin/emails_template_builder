Frequently asked questions
Is cutting my item(s) a free service?
Almost! We'll add a one-time $7.00 cutting fee to your order. (Note this will actually save you over $100 on freight charges -- see below...)
Do I have to pay for each cut?
Usually, no. Most of the time that single $7.00 fee covers all the cutting we do for your order.

In the event that you order a large number of a single item, or request multiple cuts for different items, we'll charge more than one cutting fee. After we receive your order and cutting instructions, we'll assess the amount of time and effort to fulfill your request. In some cases, we'll ask for multiple cutting fees.
Can I get a miter cut?
Unfortunately, miter cuts are really hard to get *just* right -- and we aren't great at them yet (but we're practicing!). We're happy to cut your items to length and let you make a perfect miter joint.
I don't want my item cut!
No problem! If you need this item at full length, we'll ship via common carrier as an over-sized item (charges typically range from $125-$175). We're happy to provide a freight quote -- just reply to this email or call us at {rep_phone}.

(Most of our customers prefer to have us cut for them, to save a little time and a LOT of money!)
